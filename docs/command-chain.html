Machines wired by successor pipes; material flows the way the line runs. The spinning /next drum is the sorter — the gold ↺ /next caps mark the six stations whose real successor is that drum, and the bright return main carries them back to it. One member of the successor map has no station: /start-change is injected at the head of whichever play opens a unit of work, so it rides inside those machines rather than standing on the line. The crates at the bottom sit off the line: they build the plant, they don't ride it.

# prepare-architecture

Design the project's technical architecture by reading LTM architecture knowledge, scanning the codebase, and selecting patterns, stacks, platforms, data stores, and operations infrastructure informed by the product's three-axis profiles (PP, NFR, QP). Produces a concrete `architecture.yaml` with named technologies and a `quality-standards.yaml` defining the project's quality baseline. All artifacts are stored at the product level. Requires a locked `product.yaml` (with profiles section containing all three sub-sections) and a locked `roadmap.yaml` as upstream inputs.

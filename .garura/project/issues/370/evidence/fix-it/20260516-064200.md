## Root cause

`core/components/plays/define/reference/intent.yaml` had no constraint
requiring the play to read decision manifests and surface flagged decisions
at its human checkpoints. C8 mandated manifest *emission* but nothing
mandated *surfacing*. The compiled SKILL.md faithfully reflected the gap —
Phase 5 and Phase 9 wrote a checkpoint file and an approval prompt but never
walked the manifests. Sibling plays specify/design/arch all carry the
equivalent constraint; define alone omitted it. Scope: define-local
(no shared mechanism exists).

## Fix strategy

Play-intent change per the Play Pipeline Rule. Added constraint **C17**
(Decision Surfacing Discipline) and failure condition **F11** to define's
intent.yaml, then recompiled SKILL.md via `/create-play --rebuild define`.
The regenerated play has **Step 5.0** and **Step 9.0** decision-surfacing
steps (load manifests → collect `surfaced_for_review: true` with no
`user_response` → surface batched HIGH/MID/LOW → write `user_response` back
→ then the gate) and a new step-eval **SE-13** at both phases.
